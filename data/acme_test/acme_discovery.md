## Pain Points (The "Before" State)

### Pain Point #1: Unpredictable Revenue
**Current State:** Sales forecasts are guesses (±40% accuracy). Can't predict which deals will close. Surprise revenue shortfalls quarterly. No early warning system.

**Impact:** Missed growth targets 3 years running. Can't plan hiring or inventory. CEO loses sleep over cash flow.

**Quote:** *"I find out we're missing targets when it's too late to do anything."* - Robert, CEO

### Pain Point #2: Customer Churn Mystery
**Current State:** 25% annual customer churn (industry avg: 12%). No idea which customers are at risk. Reactive customer success. No churn prediction capability.

**Impact:** Losing $2M annually to churn. Customer acquisition cost is 5x retention cost.

**Quote:** *"We lose customers and don't even know why until they're gone."* - David, Customer Success

### Pain Point #3: Inventory Chaos
**Current State:** Manual inventory tracking in spreadsheets. Stockouts on critical parts. Overstock on slow-moving items. No demand forecasting.

**Impact:** $500K in excess inventory. 15% of orders delayed due to stockouts.

**Quote:** *"We're either out of stock or drowning in inventory. No middle ground."* - Lisa, Operations

### Pain Point #4: Sales Team Flying Blind
**Current State:** No lead scoring. No insight into deal health. Sales reps waste time on dead deals.

**Impact:** Sales cycle: 6 months (industry avg: 3 months). Win rate: 15% (industry avg: 25%).
