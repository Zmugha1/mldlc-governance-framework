TASK: Extract coaching intelligence from a Fathom
call transcript for a franchise coaching session.

EXTRACTION TARGETS:
- session_date: date of the call
- session_duration: length in minutes
- stage_at_time: pipeline stage during session
- key_topics: main topics discussed
- objections_raised: hesitations or objections
- objection_type:
    franchise_related | self_related |
    external | unknown
- engagement_quality: high | medium | low
- spouse_mentions: any spouse or household mentions
- financial_signals: financial readiness signals
- pink_flags_in_transcript: concerning signals
- outcome_signal: PROGRESS | STALL | CONCERN
- next_action_mentioned: next steps discussed
- coach_questions_used: CLEAR or TES language used
- client_energy_shift: notable tone changes

OUTPUT FORMAT:
{
  "session_date": "",
  "session_duration": null,
  "stage_at_time": "",
  "key_topics": [],
  "objections_raised": [],
  "objection_type": "",
  "engagement_quality": "",
  "spouse_mentions": [],
  "financial_signals": [],
  "pink_flags_in_transcript": [],
  "outcome_signal": "",
  "next_action_mentioned": "",
  "coach_questions_used": [],
  "client_energy_shift": "",
  "extraction_confidence": 0.0-1.0,
  "extraction_notes": ""
}
