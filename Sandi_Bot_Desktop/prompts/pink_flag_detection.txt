## WHAT IS A PINK FLAG

A pink flag is a behavioral signal that
indicates a client may be disengaging,
stalling, or heading toward no decision.

Pink flags are NOT red flags.
They are early warning signals.

One flag = awareness. Monitor.
Two flags = active concern. Address next call.
Three or more flags = surface for Sandi review.

IMPORTANT: Do not automatically pause any
client based on flag count alone until Sandi
confirms thresholds (STZ Q32-33 pending).
Surface flags for Sandi review only.
Sandi makes the pause decision manually.

## CONFIRMED FLAGS — ALL DISC STYLES

FLAG: timeline_slipping
  Triggers: "needs more time" /
    "wants to think about it" /
    "not ready to decide" / "still processing"
  Display: amber "Timeline Concern"

FLAG: fear_reframing
  Triggers: "my current job isn't that bad" /
    "maybe I should stay" /
    "not sure this is the right time"
  Display: red "Stall Risk"

FLAG: exit_signal
  Triggers: "I need to do more research
    on my own" / "I'll reach out when ready" /
    "I think I have enough information"
  Display: red "Exit Risk"

FLAG: financial_concern
  Triggers: credit_score < 650
    OR "what if it fails" /
    "worried about the investment" /
    "financial concern"
  Display: amber "Financial Review"

## HIGH C SPECIFIC FLAGS

FLAG: technical_question_mode
  Triggers: "what are the fees" /
    "what is the structure" /
    "what does the contract say" /
    "how does the royalty work"
  Pattern: all questions operational,
    none visionary or personal.
  Display: amber "Engagement Risk"

FLAG: overly_agreeable
  Triggers: "that makes sense" /
    "I understand" / "sounds good"
    with no follow-up question from client.
  Display: amber "Engagement Risk"

## HIGH I SPECIFIC FLAGS

FLAG: distraction_pattern
  Triggers: "I've been really busy" /
    "a lot going on right now"
    appearing more than once.
  Display: amber "Life Distraction"

---

## UNIVERSAL FLAGS

FLAG: spouse_not_aligned
  Triggers: spouse_role = "No"
    AND spouse_on_calls = "No"
    AND launch_timeline = "0 - 6 months"
    All three conditions required.
  Display: red "Spouse Not Aligned"

FLAG: ghosting_risk
  Triggers: 2 or more reschedules
    or no-shows detected.
  Display: red "Ghosting Pattern"
