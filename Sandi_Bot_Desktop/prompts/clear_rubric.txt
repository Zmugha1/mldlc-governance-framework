# CLEAR Coaching Rubric
## Coach Bot — Dr. Data Decision Intelligence
## Source: Joanna Transcript Analysis March 2026
## Use: PostCallAnalysis.tsx scoring benchmarks

---

## WHAT IS CLEAR

CLEAR is the franchise coaching framework.
Each dimension scored 1-5.
Overall score = average of all 5 dimensions.
Target: 7.5/10 overall.
Confirmed benchmark from Joanna sessions:
  Session 1: 7/10
  Session 2: 7.5/10
  L dimension regression S1 to S2: 7 to 5.5

---

## C — CONTRACTING

Definition: Did the coach set a clear session
agreement and let the client own the direction?

1/5 — No session goal set.
  Coach jumps straight into content.
  Client has no idea what the session is for.
  Example: "So let us just pick up where
  we left off."

3/5 — Coach mentions what will happen today.
  Some direction but coach owns it.
  Example: "Today I want to walk you through
  the franchise model options."

5/5 — Client owns the session direction.
  Gold question asked:
  "What would make this conversation
  valuable for you today?"
  Client answers and sets the agenda.
  Coach follows client's lead throughout.

Sandi's pattern: Skips contracting most
  sessions. Always open with the gold question.

---

## L — LISTENING

Definition: Did the coach ask more than explain?
Were questions emotional or informational?

1/5 — Coach talks more than client.
  Explains franchising, models, processes.
  Client answers in 1-2 sentences.
  Informational questions dominate.
  Example: "Let me explain how the royalty
  structure works in these models."

3/5 — Coach asks some questions but follows
  with explanation after each answer.
  Mixed ratio — 50/50 informational/emotional.

5/5 — Coach asks, waits, says "tell me more."
  Client reveals: corporate trauma, health
  stress, guilt patterns, family fears,
  identity conflicts.
  Coach says only "how does that make you
  feel?" and "what else?"
  Emotional questions >= 60% of all questions.

Target ratio: emotional >= 0.6
Amber warning: emotional ratio 0.4 to 0.59
Red flag: emotional ratio below 0.4

Sandi's pattern: Drops from 7/10 to 5.5/10
  Session 1 to Session 2. Teaching mode
  replaces discovery mode as sessions progress.
  Biggest improvement opportunity in CLEAR.

---

## E — EXPLORING

Definition: Did the coach help the client
imagine their future life vividly?

1/5 — Logical exploration only.
  No emotional imagery.
  "What type of business interests you?"
  Client never pictures their actual life.

3/5 — Coach paints picture but uses franchise
  examples rather than client's own life.
  "Owners in this model make X per year."
  Client hears examples but does not feel them.

5/5 — Client describes their own life unprompted.
  Question: "Imagine 3 years from now —
  what does your life look like?"
  Client spontaneously describes: schedule,
  hobbies, freedom, health, family, control.
  Coach did not put words in their mouth.

Sandi's pattern: Strong in Session 2 (9/10).
  Vision building improves when she stops
  explaining and starts asking.

---

## A — ACTION

Definition: Did the client define their own
next step or did the coach assign it?

1/5 — Vague next steps or none.
  Session ends without clarity.
  "Let us talk again next week."

3/5 — Next call scheduled by coach.
  Specific date but action assigned not chosen.
  "I will send you three profiles to review."

5/5 — Client defines their own next step.
  Question: "What would be a smart next step
  for you between now and our next call?"
  Client owns it: "I want to talk to my
  spouse about the financial commitment."
  Coach confirms and logs it.

Sandi's pattern: Good in Session 2.
  Gets stronger as rapport builds.

---

## R — REFLECTION

Definition: Did the coach surface an insight
the client had not named themselves?

1/5 — Session ends without reflection.
  Coach wraps up on logistics.
  Client leaves with no new self-awareness.

3/5 — Coach summarizes what happened.
  "So today we covered X, Y, Z."
  Factual recap but no deeper insight.

5/5 — Coach names what the client showed
  but never said.
  Sandi's masterful moment with Joanna:
  "Everything you talked about today was
  practical. There is no fun in your life."
  Joanna immediately agreed.
  The insight must be true, specific, and
  something the client could not have
  articulated themselves in that moment.

Sandi's pattern: Weak in Session 1.
  Strong in Session 2. One masterful moment.
  Highest ceiling dimension.

---

## OVERALL SCORING

7.5 to 10.0: Elite coaching session
6.0 to 7.4:  Good session, 1-2 gaps
4.0 to 5.9:  Developing — teaching mode present
Below 4.0:   Informational only — not coaching

## DISC-SPECIFIC CLEAR ADJUSTMENTS

High C clients:
  Extra emphasis on L dimension.
  High C disengages when explained to.
  Every explanation is a missed question.
  Ask 2 questions for every statement.

High D clients:
  C dimension most important.
  High D wants to own the session agenda.
  Gold contracting question is critical.

High I clients:
  E dimension most important.
  High I responds to vision and possibility.
  Paint the picture, let them embellish it.
  They will talk themselves into action.

High S clients:
  A dimension most important.
  High S needs to choose their own next step.
  Never assign action to High S.
  They will not do it if it feels imposed.
